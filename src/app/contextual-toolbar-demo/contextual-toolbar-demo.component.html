Contextual Toolbar

Select the lines in the grid you want. You will see two Contextual Toolbars: one contextualized to the page, and one to the card that contains the table. Ideally, you will only place one at a time in your views.

The Contextual Toolbar will appear showing you the number of items selected, and, in this case, offering you two actions (copy and share) and one more action (delete). As this is just a demo, the actions in the toolbar will not modify the selected items, but just displayed in the console the action you selected.

You can clear the selection by using the Clear button located on the left of the Contextual Toolbar.
